Join My Lab

If you are interested in joining my lab for the fall of 2022 (or thereafter) as a graduate student, please send me an email that includes your CV and a brief description about your past research experience. If interested in joining as an undergrad, prior research experience is not required. However, for both prospective undergrad and grad students, include a few sentences about why you’re interested in joining this lab specifically.

Here’s a great resource that outlines what to include in this initial email.

More info for prospective undergraduates:

Available positions include both Work-Study (paid) and independent research projects with course credit (see course outlines page for specific course codes corresponding to 3rd and 4th year independent research projects and theses).

IMPORTANT: there will be a Biology Virtual Thesis/Research Info Night on Tuesday, October 19 from 6:30-8pm, which will provide an overview of the kinds of research being conducted in the department, and how to get involved! The event will also take place on Microsoft Teams in the “Biology Undergrad Hub” Team. Please email me if you’d like additional info.

More info for prospective graduate students:

You can apply for either a Masters or PhD (if you’ve already obtained a Masters) through the School of graduate Studies website here. Note that if you’d like to start in the fall 2022, you’d need to apply by Jan 21st (domestic students) or Jan 31st (international students) 2022, but other entry dates are possible.
